Am I Eligible to Donate?

Before you commit to the life-saving act of donation, please review the current medical and legal guidelines.

Eligibility is crucial to ensure the safety of both the donor and the recipient. If unsure, please consult a specialist.

Blood Donation Requirements

  • Age: Must be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weight: Minimum of 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Hemoglobin: Minimum level required (12.5 g/dl for women, 13.0 g/dl for men).
  • Cannot have donated in the last 8 weeks (56 days).
  • Must be free from cold, flu, fever, or active infection.
  • Cannot be taking certain medications (e.g., specific antibiotics, blood thinners).

Organ Donation Pledging

  • Age: Must be 18 years or older to legally consent.
  • Consent: Requires explicit registration and family awareness/support.
  • Medical Review: Final donation eligibility is determined by doctors at the time of death.
  • Major contraindications include HIV, active cancer, or systemic infection.
  • Specific chronic diseases (e.g., severe heart failure) may exclude some organs.
  • Pledging does not require a health check now, only your legal consent.

Ready to Make a Life-Saving Choice?

If you meet the preliminary criteria, please proceed to registration. If you have questions about specific medical conditions, contact our team.
