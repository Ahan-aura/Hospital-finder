Blood Donation: Simple Steps, Major Impact

1. Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet basic health requirements (age, weight, absence of certain conditions). We prioritize donor safety.

2. Find a Center

Locate the nearest authorized blood donation center or hospital using our portal to schedule your visit.

3. Donate & Recover

The process is quick and safe (approx. 30-45 min). Enjoy a snack and drink afterwards to replenish fluids!

Organ Donation: A Lasting Legacy

1. Register & Pledge

Fill out the official consent form online to legally register your wish to donate your organs after death.

2. Discuss with Family

Inform your family about your decision and ensure they understand your wishes regarding organ donation.

3. Save a Life

One donor can potentially save up to eight lives through the donation of vital organs and tissues.
